Welcome

Thank you for visiting my BIG Website Assignment for IT 161: Web Authoring 1, Spring Quarter 2021! Here, you will find the beginning stages of an excellent Web Developer learning the essentials in HTML and CSS. Included is a Lightbox Gallery of photos using JavaScript, a series of important research assignments, responsive Google tools applied, and a form to contact me, Jessica Dengler.

Web Development is a constant learning opportunity. there are always many ways to expand and perfect your knowledge and coding skills. It is important for me to continue to keep learning and practicing. The more experience I have under my belt, the more marketable I think I will be in the future. It is amazing how many people I know who need websites, and I can't wait to practice on them!

As HTML has progressed in the past 10 years since I was first introduced to it, I know the importance of staying on top of new trends and coding rules. By researching websites and using learning resources, I will be able to use what I've learned and apply my knowledge towards future designs and websites.

GitHub is a great resource with an infinite amount of work from peers and Web Developers alike. It is a huge source of information that is going to have a great deal of impact on my research and design ideas.
